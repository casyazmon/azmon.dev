## Investigation Underway

Teams are currently assessing the scene to determine the cause of the accident. Preliminary reports suggest that several cars left the tracks in a remote wooded area.

"Our priority is to ensure public safety and minimize environmental impact," said a spokesperson for the rail company. "We are cooperating fully with the TSB investigation."

Local authorities have secured the area, and cleanup crews are on standby to begin the removal of the derailed cars once the initial investigation is complete. This incident has once again raised questions about rail safety in the region, with local community leaders calling for stricter oversight.
